University Student Services App

UI/UX Research & Prototyping

Overview

The University Student Services App is a mobile application concept designed to simplify and improve students’ access to university services through a clean and user-friendly interface. The app focuses on enhancing usability, reducing navigation complexity, and providing students with quick access to important academic and administrative services in one centralized platform.

Tools Used

Figma

The Challenge

Many university systems are often fragmented, confusing, and difficult for students to navigate efficiently. Students struggle to quickly access essential services such as schedules, announcements, course registration, and academic information across multiple platforms. This creates frustration, wastes time, and negatively affects the overall student experience.

timer_off

Reduce Navigation Time


Students spend too much time searching for basic academic services across different systems.

psychology

Cognitive Ease


Complex interfaces and scattered information make it difficult for students to focus and complete tasks efficiently.

Design Process

search

Research

Conducted research on common challenges students face while accessing university systems, focusing on navigation difficulties, scattered services, and mobile usability issues.

architecture

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes and user flows to organize student services into a clear and intuitive structure that minimizes effort and confusion.

palette

Visual Design

Designed a modern and student-friendly interface using clean layouts, consistent spacing, and accessible visual hierarchy to improve clarity and ease of use.

The Solution

    Centralized Student Services

  • insights

  • auto_awesome
